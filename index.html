About Our Club

UNITY AND FRIENDSHIP THROUGH TENNIS

Sakumono Tennis Club (STC) was registered in 2004 as an association limited by guarantee and has been vibrant since its establishment. Located in the Tema metropolis, STC has grown to become the most reputable tennis club in Ghana. Its membership cuts across all demographic sections. In terms of membership, STC has about 130 ordinary members, 20 associate members and 5 honorary members. The Council of Patrons and the Executive Council ensure that the cherished values of friendship, Unity and togetherness are held in high esteem. STC is a proud member of the Ghana Tennis Federation and actually occupies a seat on the Ghana Tennis Federation’s regional executive council. We welcome new members to come and join the STC family where we ensure members keep healthy and ensure unity in diversity. STC has a resident coach who trains all categories of the players-from beginners, intermediate and advance players who may require brush-ups. New members can contact the secretary of the club to schedule lessons at their convenience. It is important to note that children from 6 years are also eligible to take lessons from the coach. Join us today let us play tennis!!
